OUR MISSION:



to make you a reader today and then a leader tomorrow.

We inspire people locally, nationally and
globally by extending access to Essa’s world-class teaching and
resources through flexible and inclusive opportunities,
for study and research.


OUR VISION:


To be a global centre of excellence for lifelong learning. Courses will be underpinned
by the best teaching, research and support for learning to meet the needs of diverse, ambitious
and intellectually curious students. Staff and students will work together within and beyond ESSA to
foster a vibrant learning community attentive to the importance of promoting sustainability and social justice.


OUR VALUES:


Academically excellent;


Everything we do is grounded in a commitment to academic excellence. In our teaching,
scholarship and research we draw on and share the wealth of the secondary’s unique and
world-leading resources.
